Create a premium front-end web app called Bible English.

The goal is to help me learn English by studying the Bible every day.

This is not a commercial product yet. Do not create pricing, ads, marketplace, or monetization. This is a personal study platform first.

Design Reference

Use the visual style of manuscrito.ai as the main inspiration.

Do not copy it exactly, but capture the same premium, clean, elegant, calm, minimal feeling.

The app should feel like opening a luxury Bible study edition combined with a modern Apple/Notion-style learning app.

Visual Style

Use a clean premium interface with:

deep burgundy red inspired by manuscrito.ai
warm cream background
parchment paper tones
dark leather brown
muted antique gold details
ink-black text
lots of spacing
thin borders
soft elegant cards
no emojis
no childish design
no loud gradients

Use serif typography for scripture and headings, and a clean sans-serif for interface text.

Suggested fonts:

Headings / scripture: Cormorant Garamond, EB Garamond, Libre Baskerville, or similar
Interface: Inter, SF Pro, Source Sans, or similar

Reader Page

Create a reader for John 1.

At the top of the reader, include three modes:

English Only
Bilingual
Study Mode

The selected mode should be Bilingual.

Show John 1 verses in English with the Portuguese translation below each verse.

Example:

English:
In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.

Portuguese:
No princípio era o Verbo, e o Verbo estava com Deus, e o Verbo era Deus.

The English text should be larger and darker.

The Portuguese text should be smaller, elegant, and in a warm brown tone.

Highlight the word Word in the first verse as if the user clicked it.
